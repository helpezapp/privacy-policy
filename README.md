# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: February 7, 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how our Shopping List App ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and shares information about you.

### 1. Information Collection
We do not require users to create an account or provide personal identification information (such as name or phone number) to use the core features of the app.
*   **Device Information:** We may collect basic device information (model, OS version) to improve app performance.
*   **Advertising Data:** We use Google AdMob to display ads. Google may collect advertising identifiers (like IDFA on iOS or AAID on Android) to serve personalized ads.

### 2. Use of Information
We use the information we collect to:
*   Maintain and improve the app’s functionality.
*   Show relevant advertisements to users in compliance with Google’s policies.
*   Analyze app usage trends.

### 3. Third-Party Services
Our app uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:
*   **[Google AdMob / AdSense](https://policies.google.com):** Used for displaying advertisements.
*   **[Google Analytics for Firebase](https://firebase.google.com):** Used for app performance analysis.

### 4. GDPR (European Users) & CCPA (US Users)
*   **GDPR:** Users in the EEA, UK, and Switzerland have the right to access, correct, or delete their data. You can manage your consent for personalized ads through the in-app consent dialog.
*   **CCPA:** We do not sell your personal information. You have the right to opt-out of data collection for advertising purposes.

### 5. Data Retention
Shopping lists are stored locally on your device unless otherwise synced with a cloud service you have authorized. We do not store your private lists on our servers.
